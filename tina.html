Tina

I am a happy go lucky person. The joys that your furry friends bring to me are the moments I live for. I want to provide every dog with a happy, relaxing experience so they can come home to you in the best of spirits. I live in Noda, and have been grooming professionally for 3 years. Prior to grooming I was a receptionist at a local dentist office. Having found my passion, I now fill complete and like every day is a blessing. I treat every animal as if they were my own, and ensure that they receive top notch care here at Barking Beauties. Come visit us!
